# BizBridge
BizBridge is a platform that connects customers with local small businesses that deserve more visibility.
# Features
- Business profiles
- Categories
- Search businesses
- Contact business owners
- Customer reviews
- Maps integration
- Favorites
- Mobile-friendly design
# Goal
Support local entrepreneurs by helping customers discover hidden gems in their communities.
# Description
A platform that helps people discover local small businesses while giving entrepreneurs an easy way to showcase their products and services.
